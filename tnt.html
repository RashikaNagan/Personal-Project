Tips and Tricks

The 7 effective ways to learn a piano piece: 1. Divide the piece into sections a. Using this method, not only do the smaller tasks become less stressful but also it gives a clear definition on where the next part begins. This can be done be marking on the sheet part A, B, C etc. 2. Analyse the score a. Are there any repeats? Similar parts, make sure to mark these also into the sheet 3. Hand, finger, hands a. This technique consists of 3 main parts. Firstly, ‘hand’ means to play a section of the piece with one hand. What do you notice? Is the fingering comfortable? If it isn’t, slowly go through the section with one and hand experiment with different fingerings until 1 is found comfortable. Mark this on the sheet music. Really take time to explore various options of fingerings which is comfortable for you A trick for this is to always think ahead, and think where the hand is supposed to go next. How can i go to that next part comfortably, without lfiting my hand too many times causing disruptions in the flow. 4. Work on the harder passages 5. Observe details in dynamics - Annotate the piece beforehand
